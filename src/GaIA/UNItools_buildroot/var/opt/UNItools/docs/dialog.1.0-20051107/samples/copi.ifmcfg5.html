    1 # PhoneTrans lines provide rules to change phone prefixes to make local
    2 # or long-distance calls.  In the example below, my country code is 7,
    3 # and local dialing area is 095.  From the numbers starting with "7-095-"
    4 # the prefix is stripped and the 7-digit remainder is dialed.  For
    5 # the numbers starting with "7-" but not with "7-095-", the country prefix
    6 # "7-" is stripped and the long-distance dialing prefix "8W" substituted.
    7 # For the numbers not matching any of the above, international
    8 # call is performed: international dialing prefix "8W10" is prepended
    9 # to the unmodified 11-digit number.  Generally, the syntax is:
   10 # "PhoneTrans <what-to-strip> / <what-to-substitute-instead>"
   11 #PhoneTrans      7-095-  /
   12 #PhoneTrans      7-      /       8W
   13 #PhoneTrans              /       8W10
